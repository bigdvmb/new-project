Welcome to Stututu.

Stututu is a place where you can rent Super cars and JDM. The name of our company comes from the sound of a turbo's spool.

Our cars are equipped with features including performance modifications, LB Walk's body works, and many more.

The modifications we offer range from light to heavy, perfect for drifting :)
